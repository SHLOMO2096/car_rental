## 🎓 CUSTOMIZATION GUIDE

To Modify Business Details:
   Edit: backend/app/core/signature.py
   Change: SignatureConfig class attributes
   Restart: Not needed (dynamic)

To Hide Logo in Specific Emails:
   Use: get_business_signature(include_logo=False)
   Where: In custom email functions

To Change Brand Colors:
   Edit: BRAND_COLOR_PRIMARY = "#0084C7"
   Affects: Links, headings, borders

To Add WhatsApp Link:
   Use: get_contact_link("whatsapp")
   Returns: Formatted WhatsApp link

To Disable Signature Temporarily:
   Set: include_logo=False in function call
   Or modify _base_template() temporarily
